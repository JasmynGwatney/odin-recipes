Classic Monte Cristo Sandwich by Diana Rattray

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time:10 Minutes
Serving:4 to 6

Ingredients

  • 18 Slices bread
  • Unsalted butter, to taste
  • 6 slices ham
  • 6 slices turkey, or chicken
  • Mustard, to taste, optional
  • Mayonnaise, to taste, optional
  • 12 slices Swiss or Gruy√®re cheese
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. For each sandwich use 3 slices of bread. Butter one side of bread first.

  2. Then cover with a slice of ham and a slice of turkey or chicken.

  3. Place the bread slice on top of turkey or ham slice and top with 2 thin slices of cheese.

  4. Butter the third slice for top; press sandwich lightly and trim crusts.

  5. Cut each sandwich into halves diagonally and secure with toothpicks.

  6. In a medium bowl, beat eggs with milk and salt.

  7. Heat a skillet or griddle over medium heat. Add butter to coat the pan.

  8. Carefully dip the sandwich halves into the egg mixture. Brown the sandwiches on all sides, adding more butter to the pan when necessary.

  9. Remove toothpicks before serving and serve immediately while still hot and crispy.
