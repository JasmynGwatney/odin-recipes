Loaded Breakfast Scramble by Sherry Brubaker

This one skillet Loaded Breakfast Scramble has heaps of spinach, eggs and sausage to give you the best flavors and nutrients the morning has to offer!

Prep Time:10 minutes
Cook Time:20 Minutes
Serving:5

Ingredients

  • 4 'Aidells Chicken and Apple sausages - Sliced to 1/2 in. pieces
  • 10 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 tablespoon sun-dried tomatoes
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • pinch of cracked pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
